Patricia, the adventurer

Patricia Matthews is a 18-year-old teenager who enjoys badminton, going to the movies and camping. She is smart and caring, but can also be very untrustworthy and a bit untidy. She is Guyanese who defines herself as straight. She didn't finish school. She has a severe phobia of heights Physically, Patricia is in pretty good shape. She is very short with bronze skin, red hair and blue eyes. She has asymmetrical ears. She grew up in a middle class neighbourhood.

Her mother died when she was young, she was raised by her father. She is currently in a relationship with Trinity Woodrow Slater. Trinity is 1 years older than her and works as a screenplay writer. Patricia's best friend is a teenager called Abigail Wyatt. They get on well most of the time. She also hangs around with Elaine Gray and Kerry Davies. They enjoy meditation together.
