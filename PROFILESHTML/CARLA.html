Carla, the art enthusiast

Carla Ruth Raymond is a 23-year-old art student who enjoys graphism and old french movies. She is American and spends a lot of her time at expositions. She has been exposed in multiple countries with her paintings. She grew up in a small town but moved to a big one after her first exposition.

She is currently single. She's not looking to find someone, as she feels her career is more important.

What she embodies

If art doesn’t make us better, then nothing can.
There are no mistakes. Only happy accidents.
To be an artist, you need to exist in a world of silence.
The moment you think you understand a great work of art, it’s dead for you.
