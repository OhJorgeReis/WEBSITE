Henry, the cooking chef

Henry Patricio is a 32-year-old cook who enjoys travelling and discovering new cultures. He is german and has a degree in marketing. After some years working in a publicity firm, he discovered the art of cooking and never looked back.

He opened his restaurant in his hometown and enjoys sharing the recipes he discovers in his trips with his clients.
