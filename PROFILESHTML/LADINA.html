Ladina, the party goer

Ladina Dupourqué is a 24-years-old woman who was always lucky to live in a wealthy family. She never had to work a day in her life and enjoy jumping from hotel to hotel around the globe.

Now based in Switzerland, she gets her daily rush of adrenaline by attending multiple parties during the week. She sometimes wishes she wasn't born wealthy, as she sometimes feels very disconnected with most young people her age.

What she embodies

I wish some nights lasted forever.
Dope days, chill night, good company and mellow vibes.
