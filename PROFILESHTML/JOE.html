Joe, the animal lover

Joe Cavalieri is a 32-year-old man who lives as a farmer. He was born in this same farm, and had to take control of business at the death of his father when he was 22.

He enjoys working in nature, and is in love with every one of his animals. He grew up in a space where animals were always present, and that made him very conscious of their suffering and their welfare. He only produces bio products and doesn't produce meat.
