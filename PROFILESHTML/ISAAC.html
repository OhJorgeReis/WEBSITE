Isaac, the traveler

Isaac Cardinaux is a 33-year-old man who enjoys travelling. He doesn't have a job and relies on sympathy to continue his dream of travelling the entire world. He gets on the plane with only a backpack and his guitar and tries to get some money in the countries he's visiting by singing in the streets.

He has a difficult relationship with his parents, that do believe he is wasting his life, but he does not share that opinion.

What he embodies

What's on my bucket list? Everywhere.
Life is short and the world is wide. I better get started.
My new routine: Journey. Explore. Discover. ...
Always say yes to new adventures.
