Julie, the festival enthusiast

Julie Ruth Raymond is a 30-year-old health centre receptionist who enjoys music and hanging out with friends. She is American and spends most of her summers in festivals around the world. She finished school and then left academia. She grew up in a middle class neighbourhood in Texas.

She is currently single. Her most recent romance was with a musician called Ellis Heather Yates, who was 10 years older than her. They broke up because Ellis was fed up of friends getting on better with Julie.
