Caroline, the surfer

Caroline Roberts is a 23-year-old health centre receptionist who enjoys travelling and surfing. She is energetic and reliable, but can also be very greedy. She is American and has a degree in medicine. Physically, Caroline is in pretty good shape.

She grew up in a middle class neighbourhood. Her parents separated when she was small, but remained friends and provided a happy, stable home. Caroline's best friend is a health centre receptionist called Etta Allen. They get on well most of the time.

What she embodies

You can’t buy happiness, but you can buy a surfboard and that’s pretty close.
There are a million ways to surf, but as long as you’re smiling, you’re doing it right.
When life gives you lemons, squeeze one in your hair and go surf.
