Jake, the musician

Jake Collins is a 20-years-old student in archeology in college. But his real passion is music. Nonetheless he decided to pursue a master in archeology because his mom wanted him to study something "more serious".

He has shows in local venues with his group "The Black Butterflies", and believes one day, he will have the opportunity to live from his passion.

What he embodies

No matter what you’re going through, no matter how you feel, music always helps.
All it takes is one song to bring back a thousand memories.
