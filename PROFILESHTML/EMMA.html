Emma, the cosplayer

Emma Williams is a 24-year-old lab assistant who enjoys social card games, painting and cosplaying. She is know around her small town for her excentric way of dressing. She is spanish. She finished school and then left academia. She is average-height with pale skin and brown hair. She was raised in a happy family home with two loving parents.

She adores anime and dreams of going to Japan when she'll have enough money for the plane ticket. She tries to live her japanese fantasy doing cosplay at conventions in towns around her.
