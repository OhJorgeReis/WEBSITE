Cléa and Nuno, the lovers

Cléa and Nuno are a couple of two young adults of 22 and 24 years old. Cléa is french and Nuno comes from Portugal. They met each other in Bali when they were both spending holidays there. They got close after discovering that they were both big fans of David Bowie. They now live together in Colombia and opened a motel in a forest. They enjoy nature and walks with their dog Lucky.
